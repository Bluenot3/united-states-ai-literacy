## Approved Campaign Positioning

- Audience: partners and funders.
- CTA: Partner With ZEN.
- Visual posture: civic-grade, federal-modern, dark command-interface design.
- Claim posture: no public accreditation language.
- Partner posture: no partner logos or implied government endorsement.

- ZEN AI Co. founded in 2024.
- ZEN is infrastructure, not curriculum.
- First youth AI literacy program in United States history.
- 3rd Annual AI Pioneer Program.
- Year 2 / last year baseline: 15,000 students and 12,000 apps built.
- Year 3 / current campaign metric: 30,000 students and 25,000 apps built.
- Growth: +15,000 students, +13,000 apps, +100% student growth, +108% app-output growth.
- Students ages 11-18 build cloud-hosted AI-powered agents.
- Vanguard expands the AI literacy pathway for adults and career professionals.
- Arsenal is the app, website, agent, and automation builder layer.

- AI Social and Web3 proof layers are represented as ecosystem layers, not rebuilt product claims.
